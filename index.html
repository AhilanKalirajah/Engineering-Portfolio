Process

How I approach practical engineering problems.

01

Understand the problem

Start with the production issue, operator need, drawing gap, or repair requirement before jumping into CAD.

02

Identify constraints

Review available space, equipment, safety concerns, manufacturing limits, documentation, and shop-floor realities.

03

Build CAD or layout models

Create AutoCAD layouts, SolidWorks models, drawings, or process documentation that makes the work visible and reviewable.

04

Validate the solution

Check dimensions, test fit with prototypes, compare against drawings, and review with production or quality stakeholders.

05

Support implementation

Release clear documentation and stay close to technicians, operators, and production teams as the solution moves into use.
