# Project 1 – Honda V8 Engine

## Objective

Develop a detailed 3D CAD model of a Honda V8 engine to improve proficiency with complex mechanical assemblies and advanced SolidWorks modelling techniques.

## Project Overview

The engine was modelled as a detailed assembly consisting of multiple interacting components, allowing me to better understand engine architecture, component relationships, and assembly design.

## Skills Demonstrated

- SolidWorks
- Mechanical Assembly Design
- Part Modelling
- Feature-Based CAD
- Assembly Mates
- Mechanical Components
- Engineering Visualization

## What I Learned

- Building large assemblies
- Organizing multi-part models
- Understanding engine component relationships
- Managing complex CAD features

# Project 2 – Manual Can Crusher

## Objective

Design a manually operated can crusher that converts hand force into mechanical compression through a pivoting linkage mechanism.

## Project Overview

The project focused on creating a compact mechanical device capable of crushing aluminum cans while remaining simple to manufacture and assemble.

The linkage geometry was designed to maximize mechanical advantage while maintaining smooth operation.

## Skills Demonstrated

- Mechanical Design
- Linkage Design
- SolidWorks
- Mechanism Design
- Design for Manufacturing
- Assembly Design

## Engineering Considerations

- Lever geometry
- Pivot placement
- Mechanical advantage
- Manufacturability
- Ease of assembly
- Structural simplicity

## What I Learned

- Designing moving mechanical systems
- Understanding force transmission
- Designing pivoting mechanisms
- Creating assemblies with realistic motion
